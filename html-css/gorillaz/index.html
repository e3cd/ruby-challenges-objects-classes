Who are they?

Gorillaz are a British virtual band created by musicians Damon Albarn and artist Jamie Hewlett in 1998. The band features four 2-d animated character as it's members. It's musical style is broad, melding genres such as rap, hip-hop, alternative rock and pop into it's own blend of a "postmodern" genre.
